The AI Secret Sauce

How CollabSync Works

📤

1. Ingest

Upload your notes, connect Canvas/Blackboard, or paste recent quiz scores. Our LLM engine processes everything silently.

🧬

2. Profile

Gemini 1.5 Pro extracts a Knowledge Vector ranking your proficiency in every sub-topic. E.g., Biology → Genetics → CRISPR: 82%

⚖️

3. Match

Modified Gale-Shapley algorithm maximizes total group knowledge while minimizing overlap of weak areas. Your puzzle pieces fit.

🚀

4. Collaborate

AI moderates your session in real-time — detecting confusion, generating peer-teaching prompts, and tracking learning outcomes.
